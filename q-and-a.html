Importance of SSL/HTTPS?

With more and more of our lives being automated and integrated with online applications, it is becoming best practice to encrypt sensitive information. Encrypted information will also make your application more trustworthy and in end more valuable to the end users.

Further, master Google is making SSL Certificates best practice by requiring sites to use SSL. In the end, users will be protected as well as verifying webmasters ownership of websites.

If you want to be found as well as not get screwed out of your money or personal information, SSL is a no brainer these days.

Challenges with DNS?

One of the most challenging things to understand with DNS is a nameserver. Understanding that changing nameservers can break your mail if you don't know what you are doing.

Another challenge people may face is knowing the difference between records and the name to value relationship. Knowing what a value of @ can make all the difference in the world.
